Mario's Marathon
Home How to Play
Once you understand these instructions, return to the home page and select a difficulty to begin playing. Enjoy!
Objective:

- Help Mario avoid running into the green pipes for as long as possible.

How to play:

- Click and/or hold any key to navigate upwards and release all keys for Mario to fall back down.

Other instructions:

- To restart the game, press the purple "New Game" button below the game.

- To select a new difficulty, return to the home page and do so.
